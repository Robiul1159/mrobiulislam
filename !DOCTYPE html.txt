<section id="projects">
    <h2>Research Projects</h2>

    <h3>Wearable Triboelectric Sensors</h3>
    <p>
        Development of flexible and highly sensitive triboelectric sensors
        for pulse monitoring, respiration detection, sign-language
        recognition, and human–machine interaction.
    </p>
